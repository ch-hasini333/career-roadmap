## 🚀 Overview
Instead of long explanations, this website focuses on main keywords, structure, and clarity, helping students avoid confusion and understand the bigger picture.
Specially designed for Tier-2 and Tier-3 engineering students, who often lack proper guidance about industry pathways.
It explains what to learn, why to learn, and how everything connects, so students can move in the right direction without wasting time.
This platform converts confusion → clarity → career direction 💯`
